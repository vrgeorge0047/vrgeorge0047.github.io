Revamping my story telling website.

For long, I have been craving to build my story telling website. With my lack of knowledge in css, it took me some time to create from scratch my idea of a very simple story telling website. There are many who wishes to read, even when there are other multimedia options available. 

I am sharing here how my site looked initially, and how it was changed drastically by the use of a tool - PageFlip 5.

NB:- Still in development

A very basic html design, not fully responsive, yet functional. Not fancy enough to my likes.

This is my original design, made up with html and with my very minimal knowledge in css. This is still in development, and I was not liking the lack of fanciness that attracts everyone alike.

This design simple navigates to the next page when clicking anywhere inside the div. The users can also scroll back and forth with mouse, or using touch gestures.

Introduction to PageFlip, Intiutive design, Horizontal scrolling with interactive controls, Simulates realistic page flipping action

I have been looking for a nice and fancy way to convert my design to something more interactive and that looks like flipping pages of a book.

I had literally dropped the idea of going ahead with my story telling website, as I was not able to make it looks cool and interactive. The existing back and forth page flow and the lack of interactive buttons have made me decide to slow down.

A few days back, I was searching for website templates with horizontal scrolling and navigation buttons to interact with, when the search result with PageFlip showed up.

I very much liked the demo scenes shown in the PageFlip website located at https://pageflip-books.com/

I asked for a 14 day trial key, which was provided to me via email. This is the result of the development progress.
